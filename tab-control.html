Tab Control

Why use an Tab Control?

  • Tab Controls can condense a short list of terms and/or concepts into a smaller area.
  • You have very few, single word terms with longer descriptions.
  • Tab Controls isolate each concept and it's explanation or definition, aiding retention.
  • Terms & definitions are perfect for an tab control.

Why not an Tab Control?

  • Your terms are more than a single word or very short phrase.
  • You have more than 3 term/definition pairs.
  • The learner is expected to compare or contrast terms.
